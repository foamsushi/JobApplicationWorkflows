## Status Legend

- `To Do`: Found, haven't started.
- `Drafting`: Agent is generating analysis/documents.
- `Applied`: Documents sent.
- `Interviewing`: Phone screen or onsites.
- `Offer`: Success!
- `Archived`: Rejected, withdrawn, or decided not to apply.
