### **Rule Set 6: The "Negative Parallelism" Ban**

Wikipedia editors explicitly flag **Negative Parallelisms**—the "Not X, but Y" sentence structure—as a sign of AI.1

* **The Artifact:** "It is not merely a tool, but a partner." "This is not just a building, but a testament to..." "The goal is not only to survive, but to thrive."  
* **The Mechanism:** This is a rhetorical device that sounds "smart" and "insightful." AI overuses it to generate "synthetic insight" where none exists.  
* **The Human Rule:** **State what the thing IS, not what it isn't.** unless you are actually correcting a misconception.  
  * *AI:* "It is not merely a phone, but a portal to the world."  
  * *Human:* "The phone connects you to the world."
