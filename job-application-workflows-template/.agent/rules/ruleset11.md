### **Rule Set 11: The "Dear User" Artifacts**

AI often hallucinates that it is writing a letter or an email, even when asked for an article.1

* **The Artifact:**  
  * "Subject lines" at the top of an article.  
  * "I hope this helps\!" at the end.  
  * "Collaborative communication" (e.g., "Let's explore...").  
* **The Human Rule:** **Remove the Meta-Commentary.** The text should exist as a standalone object. Remove the "I" (unless it's a personal essay) and the "You" (direct address) if the format doesn't call for it.
