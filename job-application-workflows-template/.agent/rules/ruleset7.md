### **Rule Set 7: The Transition Purge (The "Glue" Removal)**

AI models are terrified of silence between sentences. They feel a probabilistic pressure to explicitly signal the logical relationship between every thought. This leads to the overuse of **transitional adverbs**.10

* **The Artifact:** The constant use of:  
  * "Moreover"  
  * "Furthermore"  
  * "Additionally"  
  * "Consequently"  
  * "Notably"  
  * "Importantly"  
* **The Insight:** "Nobody uses transitions" like this in natural writing.10 Humans rely on the *content* to carry the flow.  
* **The Human Rule:** **Delete 90% of transition words.**  
  * *Test:* Read the two sentences without the transition. Does it still make sense?  
  * *AI:* "The market is crashing. Furthermore, inflation is rising."  
  * *Human:* "The market is crashing. Inflation is rising." (The link is implied).
