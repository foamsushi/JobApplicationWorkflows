### **Rule Set 8: The "Conclusion" Amputation**

AI models have a deep-seated bias toward "wrapping up." Even in short texts, they try to add a summary paragraph starting with "In conclusion," "Ultimately," or "In summary".9

* **The Artifact:** A final paragraph that restates what was just said, often adding a moralizing gloss ("...reminding us of the importance of...").  
* **The Human Rule:** **Stop when you are done.** Do not summarize unless the document is over 2,000 words.  
  * *Protocol:* Delete the last paragraph of any AI draft. Usually, the text is stronger without it.
