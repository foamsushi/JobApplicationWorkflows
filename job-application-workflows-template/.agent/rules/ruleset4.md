### **Rule Set 4: The Copula Restoration (The "Is" Rule)**

One of the most subtle but pervasive signs of AI writing identified by Wikipedia forensics is the **Avoidance of Basic Copulatives** (the "is/are" gap).1

* **The Mechanism:** AI models are often fine-tuned to avoid "passive voice" or "weak verbs" (like *to be*) based on stylistic guidelines in their training set. They over-correct, leading to complex sentence structures that avoid simple assertions.  
* **The Artifact:** Instead of saying "X is Y," the AI says "Serving as X, Y acts as..." or "X, characterized by its Y-ness..."  
  * *Snippet Example:* "Thus the highly specific 'inventor of the first train-coupling device' might become 'a revolutionary titan of industry'"—but structurally, it also becomes "Operating as a pivotal figure...".1  
* **The Human Rule:** **Embrace the verb "To Be."** It is the most honest verb in the language.  
  * *AI Style:* "The software, acting as a bridge between systems, facilitates data transfer." (Participial phrase front-loading).  
  * *Human Style:* "The software **is** a bridge between systems. It transfers data."  
  * *Protocol:* Scan your text for sentences starting with "-ing" verbs (Participles). Rewrite them as simple Subject-Verb sentences.
