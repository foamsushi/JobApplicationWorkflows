### **Rule Set 2: The "Peacocking" Prohibition**

A major sign of AI writing on Wikipedia is "Undue emphasis on notability".9 The AI tries to convince the reader that the subject is important by using adjectives rather than facts.

* **The Artifact:** "John Doe is a *renowned visionary* in the field of..."  
* **The Human Rule:** **Kill the Adjectives.** Trust the Noun.  
  * *AI:* "He is a celebrated master of the culinary arts."  
  * *Human:* "He is a chef who won three Michelin stars."  
  * *Analysis:* The AI version is "fading from a sharp photograph into a blurry, generic sketch".1 The human version restores the sharpness.
