### **Rule Set 5: The "Rule of Three" Breaker**

AI has a mathematical obsession with the **Rule of Three** (triads). When listing attributes, reasons, or examples, LLMs default to three items to create a sense of rhythm and completeness.1

* **The Artifact:** "The solution is robust, scalable, and secure." "He was known for his courage, his wisdom, and his integrity."  
* **The Human Rule:** **Disrupt the Triad.** Humans are rarely so rhythmically perfect.  
  * *Protocol:* Use two items. Or four. Or one very detailed item.  
  * *Human Revision:* "The solution is scalable and secure." (Two items).  
  * *Human Revision:* "He was known for his courage." (One item).  
  * *Why:* This breaks the "hypnotic" rhythm of AI prose and increases "burstiness" (variance).
