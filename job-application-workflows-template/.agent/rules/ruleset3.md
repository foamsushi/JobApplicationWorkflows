### **Rule Set 3: The "Vibrant" Filtering**

Wikipedia editors have noted a specific obsession with the word "vibrant" to describe "cultural heritage" or "cities".8 This is part of a cluster of "positive sentiment" words including "rich," "deeply rooted," and "nestled in the heart of."

* **The Human Rule:** If you are describing a place or culture, and you reach for the word "vibrant," stop. Force yourself to name *one specific element* that creates the vibrancy. Is it the music? The traffic? The colors? Write that instead.
