### **Rule Set 10: The Title Case Correction**

AI often applies **Title Case** to section headers where Sentence case would be standard (especially in Wikipedia or academic contexts).1

* **The Artifact:** "\#\# The Signs Of AI Writing"  
* **The Human Rule:** **Use Sentence case.** "\#\# The signs of AI writing." It looks less corporate and more editorial.
