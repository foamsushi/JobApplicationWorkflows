### **Phase 1: The Purge (Delete)**

1. **CTRL+F "Delve", "Foster", "Tapestry", "Realm", "Landscape".** Delete or replace immediately.
2. **Delete the Introduction:** If the first paragraph is generic "scene-setting" (e.g., "In today's fast-paced world..."), delete it. Start with the second paragraph.
3. **Delete the Conclusion:** If the last paragraph starts with "In conclusion" or summarizes the obvious, delete it.
4. **Delete Transitions:** Remove "Moreover," "Furthermore," "Additionally."

### **Phase 2: The Disruption (Rewrite)**

5. **Break the Lists:** Convert "Bold Header" lists into narrative paragraphs.
6. **Break the Rhythm (Burstiness):** Look for three sentences of similar length. Combine two of them into a long, complex sentence. Chop one into a short fragment.
7. **Fix the Copulas:** Find sentences starting with "-ing" phrases. Rewrite them as "Subject IS Object."
8. **Fix the Triads:** Find lists of three adjectives. Delete one or add a fourth.

### **Phase 3: The Injection (Add)**

9. **Add Specificity:** Find every adjective (e.g., "vibrant"). Replace it with a fact (e.g., "noisy").
10. **Add "Anti-Hedges":** Find "It is important to note." Delete it. Make the statement directly.
11. **Add "I" (If applicable):** If the format allows, inject a personal opinion or experience. AI cannot have experiences.
12. **Add "Roughness":** Use a colloquialism, a rhetorical question, or a strong opinion. Break the "neutral point of view" unless you are actually writing for Wikipedia.
    Allowed Roughness: Strong opinions about methods, Clear trade-offs, First-person ownership
    Disallowed Roughness: Casual language about safety, customers, OEMs, Slang, Humor
